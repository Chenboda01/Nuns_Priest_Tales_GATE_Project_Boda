As the fox ran, Chanticleer called out, "Sir Fox, I know you've caught me — but before you eat me, wouldn't you like to shout back at those foolish hens and tell them you've won?" The proud fox opened his mouth to gloat, and in that instant, Chanticleer flew up into a tall tree! "No, no, no!" Chanticleer called down. "You won't trick me with flattery a second time. If a rooster closes his eyes when he should keep them open, he deserves what happens!"
