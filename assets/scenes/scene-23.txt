Chanticleer strutted into the yard, proud and royal, no longer afraid at all. He marched up and down on his toes like a king. Whenever he found a grain of corn, he called "chuck, chuck, chuck!" and all his hens came running as fast as they could. It was a beautiful May morning, and Chanticleer looked at the bright sun, the singing birds, and the fresh flowers all around. He was the happiest rooster alive!
